<p><p>Electrical Discharge Machining (EDM) is a manufacturing process that employs electrical discharges or sparks to remove material from a workpiece. The three primary market types are Sinker EDM, which shapes materials by using a custom-shaped electrode; Wire EDM, which cuts materials along a predetermined path using a thin wire; and Hole Drilling EDM, designed for creating precise holes in hard materials. Each type serves specific industrial applications, emphasizing precision, efficiency, and the ability to work with complex geometries.</p></p>
