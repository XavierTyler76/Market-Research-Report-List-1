<p><p>Machine tool steel is categorized based on its application and properties. **Hot-work steel** is designed for high-temperature environments, maintaining hardness and strength during prolonged heat exposure. **Cold-work steel** excels in low-temperature conditions, offering high wear resistance and toughness for cutting and shaping. **Plastics mold steel** is tailored for molding plastics, providing excellent polishability and corrosion resistance. **High-speed steel** is known for its ability to retain hardness at high temperatures, ideal for cutting tools. **Others** include specialized steels for unique industrial needs, balancing durability and performance.</p></p>
