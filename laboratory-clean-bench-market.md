<p><p>Laboratory clean benches are specialized workspaces designed to provide a controlled environment. The UV clean bench utilizes ultraviolet light to sterilize surfaces, effectively eliminating pathogens. HEPA filter clean benches employ High-Efficiency Particulate Air filters to trap airborne contaminants, ensuring a sterile workspace. Vertical laminar flow clean benches direct filtered air vertically, minimizing particle contamination in sensitive applications. Other types may include portable models or those with specific features tailored to unique lab requirements, catering to diverse research and operational needs.</p></p>
