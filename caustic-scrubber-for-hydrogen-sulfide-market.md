<p><p>Caustic scrubbers for hydrogen sulfide (H2S) are essential for gas purification, employing various designs. Vertical scrubbers, also known as counter-flow scrubbers, facilitate upward gas flow against descending caustic liquid, ensuring efficient mass transfer and higher removal efficiency. In contrast, horizontal scrubbers, or cross-flow scrubbers, allow gas to flow horizontally while the liquid moves perpendicular to the gas, offering a compact design suitable for space-constrained applications. Both types provide effective H2S removal, catering to different industrial needs.</p></p>
