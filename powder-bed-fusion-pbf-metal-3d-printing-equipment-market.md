<p><p>Powder Bed Fusion (PBF) metal 3D printing equipment is categorized into single laser and multiple laser systems, each offering distinct advantages. Single laser machines utilize one focused laser beam to fuse metal powders layer by layer, ideal for producing small to medium-sized parts with precision. In contrast, multiple laser systems employ several lasers simultaneously, significantly increasing production speed and enabling the fabrication of larger and more complex geometries. This duality caters to diverse industry needs, from prototyping to high-volume manufacturing.</p></p>
