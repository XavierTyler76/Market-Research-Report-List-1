<p><strong>The Colonic Irrigation Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open System Machine</li><li>Close System Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The colonic irrigation devices market is categorized into open system and closed system machines. Open system machines involve gravity-fed systems where water flows through a colon tube, allowing for a more natural cleansing process. Closed system machines, on the other hand, utilize a pressurized mechanism to control water flow and waste removal, providing a more contained and hygienic experience. Each type offers distinct benefits, catering to various preferences for safety, comfort, and effectiveness in cleansing procedures.</p></p>
