<p><p>The petrochemical catalyst market encompasses various types tailored for specific processes. FCC (Fluid Catalytic Cracking) catalysts optimize crude oil conversion into lighter products. Hydrotreating catalysts remove impurities like sulfur and nitrogen from fuels. Hydrocracking catalysts break heavy hydrocarbons into lighter, valuable products. Polymerization catalysts facilitate the bonding of monomers into polymers. Alkylation catalysts enhance gasoline octane by combining light olefins and isobutane. Reforming catalysts convert low-octane naphtha into high-octane aromatics. Other catalysts cater to niche processes, ensuring efficiency and product quality across the petrochemical industry.</p></p>
