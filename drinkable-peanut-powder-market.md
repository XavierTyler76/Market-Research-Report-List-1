<p><ul><li>Soluble (Powder)</li><li>Insoluble (Particle)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drinkable peanut powder market is primarily divided into two types: soluble and insoluble. Soluble peanut powder is finely processed to dissolve quickly in liquids, making it ideal for smoothies, shakes, and beverages. It provides a creamy texture and rich flavor without grittiness. In contrast, insoluble peanut powder contains larger particles that do not dissolve completely, offering a more robust peanut taste and a thicker consistency in drinks, catering to consumers who prefer a heartier texture and flavor.</p></p>
