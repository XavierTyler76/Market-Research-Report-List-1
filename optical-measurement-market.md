<p><strong>The Optical Measurement Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Autocollimators</li><li>Measuring Microscopes</li><li>Profile Projectors</li><li>Optical Digitizers and Scanners (ODSs)</li><li>Coordinate Measuring Machines (CMMs)</li><li>Video Measuring Machines (VMMs)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical measurement market encompasses various types of technologies aimed at precise measurement and inspection. Autocollimators are used for angular measurements, while measuring microscopes provide high-resolution imaging for micro-scale dimensions. Profile projectors project magnified images for profile analysis. Optical digitizers and scanners (ODSs) convert physical objects into digital formats. Coordinate measuring machines (CMMs) measure the physical geometrical characteristics of objects using probes, and video measuring machines (VMMs) utilize cameras for non-contact measurements, offering speed and accuracy in quality control processes.</p></p>
