<p><p>The steep incline conveyor market comprises two main types: steep slope belt conveyors and steep slope chain conveyors. Steep slope belt conveyors utilize flexible belts to transport materials at steep angles, perfect for industries needing efficient elevation, like mining and construction. Conversely, steep slope chain conveyors rely on durable chains for robust material handling, making them suitable for heavy, abrasive materials. Both types enhance space efficiency and material flow, catering to diverse industrial applications where vertical transport is essential.</p></p>
