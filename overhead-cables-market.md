<p><ul><li>Low Voltage Cables</li><li>High Voltage Cables</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The overhead cables market is segmented into low voltage and high voltage cables. Low voltage cables are designed for distribution in residential and commercial applications, typically operating below 1kV, providing safety and efficiency. In contrast, high voltage cables cater to transmission networks, operating above 1kV, facilitating the long-distance transfer of electricity from power plants to substations. Both types play crucial roles in power distribution, with low voltage cables ensuring accessibility and high voltage cables enabling extensive connectivity across regions.</p></p>
