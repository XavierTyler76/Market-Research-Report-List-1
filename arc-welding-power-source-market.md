<p><p>The arc welding power source market consists of various equipment types designed for specific welding processes. Stick equipment utilizes a consumable electrode to create a molten weld pool, ideal for outdoor and overhead applications. TIG (Tungsten Inert Gas) equipment offers high precision with a non-consumable electrode, suitable for thin materials. MIG/MAG (Metal Inert/Active Gas) equipment provides a fast and efficient welding process using a continuous wire feed. Multi-process equipment combines these functionalities, allowing versatility for different welding tasks in various industries.</p></p>
