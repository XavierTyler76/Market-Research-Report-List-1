<p><ul><li>Mono Pump</li><li>Dual Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The windshield washer pump market primarily features mono pumps and dual pumps. Mono pumps consist of a single motor unit, designed for basic windshield cleaning functions, pushing fluid from the reservoir to the windshield. In contrast, dual pumps include two separate units, enabling enhanced performance by providing more fluid pressure and allowing simultaneous operation for front and rear windshields. The dual pump type is preferred in vehicles requiring advanced cleaning capabilities, while mono pumps are commonly found in standard vehicles.</p></p>
