Moveable barrier systems are adaptable, allowing for adjustments in positioning to manage traffic flow and improve safety during roadworks or events. In contrast, immovable barrier systems are fixed installations designed to protect vehicles and pedestrians from hazards, providing robust physical separation on roads. Both systems aim to enhance road safety, mitigate collision impacts, and reduce injury severity in automotive accidents.</p></p>
