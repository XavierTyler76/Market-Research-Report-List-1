<p><strong>The Oil Casing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Casing</li><li>Technical Casing</li><li>Oil Layer Casing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil casing market consists of three main types: surface casing, technical casing, and oil layer casing. Surface casing is installed to protect groundwater and provides structural integrity to the wellhead. Technical casing, often referred to as intermediate casing, ensures stability in challenging geological conditions and facilitates safe drilling. Oil layer casing is set within the productive oil zone, designed to contain oil and gas, preventing reservoir pressure loss and enhancing extraction efficiency. These casings are crucial for safe and effective oil exploration and production.</p></p>
