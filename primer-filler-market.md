<p><p>The Primer Filler market includes One-Component and Two-Component types. One-Component Primer Fillers are pre-mixed, easy to apply, and ideal for quick, small-scale projects due to their simplicity and shorter drying times. Two-Component Primer Fillers consist of a base and a hardener, requiring mixing before use. They offer superior durability, adhesion, and resistance, making them suitable for demanding, large-scale applications. Both types enhance surface preparation, ensuring smooth finishes and improved paint adhesion, catering to different project needs and complexities.</p></p>
