<p><ul><li>Wet Dust Suppression Agents</li><li>Dry Dust Suppression Agents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dust suppression agents market is divided into two main types: wet and dry dust suppression agents. Wet dust suppression agents, which include liquids like water, polymers, and surfactants, are commonly used to minimize airborne dust by increasing moisture levels in the environment. Dry dust suppression agents, on the other hand, consist of granular materials like calcium chloride and gypsum that absorb moisture and bind dust particles. Both types play a crucial role in improving air quality and enhancing safety in various industrial applications.</p></p>
