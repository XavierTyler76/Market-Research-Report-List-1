<p><p>The Logic Test Probe Card market encompasses three primary types: Vertical Needle/Tip, Thin Film MLO, and Membrane Probe Cards. Vertical Needle/Tip probe cards utilize rigid needles for direct contact with chip pads, offering precision. Thin Film MLO probe cards feature a thin film substrate with multiple layers, enhancing performance and density in testing. Membrane probe cards utilize a flexible membrane to adapt to varying chip topographies, ensuring reliable contact and improved testing accuracy in logic devices. Each type caters to different testing requirements in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
