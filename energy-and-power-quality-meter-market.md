<p><p>The energy and power quality meter market comprises three primary types: detached type, portable type, and embedded type. Detached type meters are standalone devices installed at fixed locations, often used for permanent monitoring. Portable type meters are designed for mobility, allowing users to measure power quality across different sites as needed. Embedded type meters are integrated into electrical systems or equipment, providing real-time data and analysis directly within the operating environment, enhancing monitoring without requiring separate devices.</p></p>
