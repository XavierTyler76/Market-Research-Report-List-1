<p><p>The cardiac bio implant devices market includes various types of devices that support heart function and address cardiovascular conditions. Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs) provide life-saving shocks to restore normal heart rhythm. Implantable Cardiac Pacemakers (ICPs) regulate heartbeats for those with arrhythmias. Heart valves replace damaged valves to improve blood flow. Coronary stents maintain vessel patency, while peripheral stents are used in peripheral arteries. Ventricular Assist Devices support heart pumping in severe heart failure cases, enhancing patient outcomes and quality of life.</p></p>
