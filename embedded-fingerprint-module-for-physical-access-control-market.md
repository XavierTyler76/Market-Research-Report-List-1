<p><strong>The Embedded Fingerprint Module for Physical Access Control Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive Fingerprint Module</li><li>Optical Fingerprint Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Embedded fingerprint modules for physical access control are classified into two main types: capacitive and optical fingerprint modules. Capacitive fingerprint modules use electrical capacitance to capture the unique patterns of a fingerprint, offering high accuracy and durability, making them suitable for secure environments. In contrast, optical fingerprint modules utilize light to scan and create a digital image of the fingerprint, generally being easier and more cost-effective to implement but may offer lower security in highly sensitive applications.</p></p>
