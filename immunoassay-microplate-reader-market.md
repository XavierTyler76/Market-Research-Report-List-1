<p><p>The Immunoassay Microplate Reader market encompasses various detection types. **Absorbance Detection** measures light absorption by samples, ideal for ELISA assays. **Colorimetric Detection** quantifies color changes in reactions, commonly used in biochemical assays. **Fluorescence Detection** identifies fluorescent labels, offering high sensitivity for low-concentration analytes. **Luminescence Detection** measures light emitted from chemical reactions, suitable for high-throughput screening. **LED Detection** uses light-emitting diodes for cost-effective, energy-efficient readings. **Other** methods include advanced or hybrid technologies catering to specialized applications, enhancing versatility and precision in immunoassay analysis.</p></p>
