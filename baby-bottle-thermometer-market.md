<p><p>The baby bottle thermometer market is divided into two main types: non-contact infrared and contact thermometers. Non-contact infrared baby bottle thermometers measure the temperature of the milk without direct contact, offering quick and hygienic results. They are ideal for parents seeking convenience and speed. In contrast, contact baby bottle thermometers require placement on the bottle or milk for temperature readings, providing direct measurement. These are often favored for accuracy but may take slightly longer. Both types cater to parental needs for safe feeding.</p></p>
