<p><p>The N-Type solar cell market is characterized by advanced technologies such as Heterojunction (HJT) cells, Tunnel Oxide Passivated Contact (TOPCon) cells, and Interdigitated Back Contact (IBC) cells. HJT cells combine crystalline and thin-film technologies, offering high efficiency and better temperature performance. TOPCon cells utilize a passivation layer to enhance efficiency and reduce recombination losses. IBC cells feature contacts on the rear side, maximizing light absorption and improving performance. Each technology aims to enhance efficiency and lower costs in solar energy generation.</p></p>
