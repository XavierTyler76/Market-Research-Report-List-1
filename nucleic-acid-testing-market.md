<p><strong>The Nucleic Acid Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transcription-mediated Amplification (TMA)</li><li>Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR)</li><li>Ligase Chain Reaction (LCR)</li><li>Whole Genome Sequencing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nucleic acid testing market comprises several key technologies. Transcription-mediated amplification (TMA) utilizes enzymatic reactions to amplify RNA targets, enhancing sensitivity for viral detection. Polymerase chain reaction (PCR) is widely used for DNA amplification, offering rapid results and versatility in applications. Ligase chain reaction (LCR) employs DNA ligation for specific target amplification, ensuring high fidelity and sensitivity. Whole genome sequencing enables comprehensive analysis of entire genomes, facilitating genomics research and personalized medicine. Each method addresses specific diagnostic needs in molecular biology.</p></p>
