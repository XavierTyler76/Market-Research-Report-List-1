<p><p>The gas-welding hose market is segmented primarily into Grade R and Grade T types. Grade R hoses are designed for general-purpose gas welding and cutting applications, featuring a moderate flexibility and resistance to various gases. They are suitable for acetylene and oxygen service. In contrast, Grade T hoses are intended for high-performance applications, providing enhanced flexibility and superior resistance to heat and abrasion. These features make Grade T hoses ideal for more demanding environments and advanced welding processes.</p></p>
