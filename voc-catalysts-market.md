<p><ul><li>Pellet</li><li>Honeycomb</li><li>Columnar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VOC catalysts market comprises three main types: pellet, honeycomb, and columnar. Pellet catalysts are small, spherical particles that provide a high surface area for reactions, making them effective for volatile organic compound (VOC) treatment. Honeycomb catalysts feature a porous, structured design that facilitates gas flow and enhances reaction efficiency. Columnar catalysts are presented in cylindrical or tubular forms, allowing for organized flow paths and even distribution of reactants. Each type is tailored to optimize VOC removal in various industrial applications.</p></p>
