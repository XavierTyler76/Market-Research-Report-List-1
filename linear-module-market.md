<p><ul><li>Ball Screw Linear Module</li><li>Synchronous Belt Linear Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Linear modules are essential components in automation and precision machinery, categorized mainly into ball screw linear modules and synchronous belt linear modules. Ball screw linear modules utilize a screw-driven mechanism, providing high accuracy and load capacity, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, synchronous belt linear modules operate using belts and pulleys, offering faster movement and smoother operation, suited for lighter loads and higher speeds. Both types cater to diverse industrial automation needs, enhancing efficiency and precision in various applications.</p></p>
