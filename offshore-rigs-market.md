<p><p>The offshore rigs market comprises various types designed for drilling and production activities in marine environments. Jackups are self-elevating platforms with legs that rest on the seabed, suited for shallow waters. Semisubmersibles are buoyant structures that float and are anchored to the seabed, ideal for deep waters. Drill ships are specialized vessels equipped for deep-water drilling, offering mobility and flexibility. Other types may include tension-leg platforms and spars, catering to specific operational and environmental needs in offshore exploration and production.</p></p>
