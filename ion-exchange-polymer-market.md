<p><ul><li>Anion Ion-exchange Polymer</li><li>Cation Ion-exchange Polymer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ion-exchange polymer market is categorized into two main types: anion and cation ion-exchange polymers. Anion ion-exchange polymers are designed to exchange negatively charged ions, effectively removing contaminants such as nitrates and heavy metals from water and other solutions. In contrast, cation ion-exchange polymers target positively charged ions, facilitating the removal of substances like calcium and magnesium, which can cause hardness in water. Both types are crucial in applications ranging from water treatment to food and beverage processing.</p></p>
