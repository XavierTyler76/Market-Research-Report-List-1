<p><p>An Automatic Voltage Regulator (AVR) ensures stable voltage levels for electrical equipment by automatically adjusting the output voltage. The AVR market is categorized into Linear Regulators, which provide smooth voltage regulation with low noise and are best for low-power applications; Shunt Voltage Regulators, which maintain a specified output voltage by diverting excess current and are commonly used in automotive applications; and Other types, which include switching regulators and hybrid systems designed for specialized applications, enhancing flexibility in voltage management.</p></p>
