<p><p>The industrial heating furnace market is primarily categorized into two types: combustion and electric. Combustion-type furnaces utilize fuels such as natural gas, oil, or coal to generate heat, making them suitable for high-temperature applications and large-scale operations. In contrast, electric-type furnaces rely on electric currents to produce heat, offering precise temperature control and cleaner operation, ideal for smaller or specialized tasks. Both types cater to diverse industries, including metal processing, glass manufacturing, and ceramics.</p></p>
