<p><strong>The Radiation Hardened Optical Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode Fiber</li><li>Multimode Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Radiation Hardened Optical Fiber market encompasses two primary types: Single Mode Fiber and Multimode Fiber. Single Mode Fiber is designed for long-distance communication, offering low attenuation and high data rates, making it suitable for critical applications in environments with high radiation exposure, such as space or nuclear facilities. In contrast, Multimode Fiber is typically used for shorter distances and has a larger core, allowing multiple light paths, making it effective for local area networks in less radiation-sensitive applications. Both fibers are engineered to withstand harsh radiation environments.</p></p>
