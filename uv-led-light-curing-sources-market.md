<p><ul><li>Spot Light Source</li><li>Line Light Source</li><li>Surface Light Source</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV LED light curing sources market comprises three main types: spot light sources, line light sources, and surface light sources. Spot light sources focus intense UV light on a specific area, ideal for precision curing applications. Line light sources emit UV light in a linear fashion, suitable for curing processes on wider surfaces or in continuous manufacturing lines. Surface light sources provide even UV illumination across larger areas, enhancing efficiency in curing coatings or adhesives on flat substrates.</p></p>
