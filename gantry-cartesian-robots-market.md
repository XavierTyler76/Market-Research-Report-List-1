Cartesian robots, on the other hand, operate on linear rails with three linear axes (X, Y, and Z), and are commonly deployed in applications such as packaging, pick and place operations, and CNC machining due to their straightforward design and ease of integration.</p><p>The Gantry and Cartesian Robots Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.8% during the forecast period.
