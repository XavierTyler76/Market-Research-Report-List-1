<p><p>Thermal mass flow meters are devices used to measure the mass flow rate of gases and fluids based on the principle of heat transfer. They function by detecting changes in temperature as a gas flows over a heated element, allowing for precise measurement of gas flow without the need for additional pressure or temperature corrections. These instruments are widely utilized in various industries, including oil and gas, chemical processing, and environmental monitoring, due to their accuracy and reliability.</p><p>The Thermal Mass Flow Meters Market is anticipated to grow significantly, with an expected CAGR of 9.70% during the forecast period.
