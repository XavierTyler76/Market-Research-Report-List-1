<p><p>The Polyphenylene Sulfone (PPSU) resins market encompasses two primary types: powder PPSU and granular PPSU. Powder PPSU is characterized by its fine particulate form, ideal for applications requiring high thermal stability and chemical resistance, such as medical devices and aerospace components. In contrast, granular PPSU features larger pellets, facilitating easy processing through injection molding and extrusion. Both forms are increasingly utilized across industries, driven by their superior mechanical properties and versatility in high-performance applications.</p></p>
