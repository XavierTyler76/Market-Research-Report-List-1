<p><p>The enzyme preparations market is categorized into six main types based on their catalytic functions. Oxidoreductases facilitate oxidation-reduction reactions, while Transferases transfer functional groups between substrates. Hydrolases catalyze hydrolysis reactions, breaking down compounds with water. Isomerases are responsible for structural rearrangements within molecules. Lyases remove groups from, or add groups to, molecules without hydrolysis, whereas Ligases join two molecules using ATP energy. Each type plays a critical role across various industries, including pharmaceuticals, food, and biotechnology.</p></p>
