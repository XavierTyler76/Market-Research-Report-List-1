<p><p>The Pediatric Audiometry Devices market includes various types used to assess hearing in children. Diagnostic audiometers are advanced tools that provide detailed evaluations of hearing ability, enabling precise diagnosis of hearing disorders. Screening audiometers are portable devices designed for quick assessments to identify potential hearing issues in children during routine checks. Acoustic reflex testers measure the middle ear's response to sound, helping to evaluate ear function. Other devices may include tympanometers and OAE (otoacoustic emissions) testers, broadening diagnostic capabilities.</p></p>
