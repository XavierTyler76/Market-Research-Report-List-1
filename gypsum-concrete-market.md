<p><p>Gypsum concrete is a versatile material used in construction for flooring and leveling. It comes in three main market types: Powder, Premix, and Paste. Powder gypsum concrete is a dry blend of gypsum and additives, requiring mixing with water on-site. Premix is a ready-to-use formulation, combining gypsum, aggregates, and binders for convenience. Paste is a pre-mixed, flowable form, ideal for precise applications like underfloor heating systems. Each type caters to specific construction needs, balancing ease of use and performance.</p></p>
