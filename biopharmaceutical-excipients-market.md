<p><p>The biopharmaceutical excipients market comprises several key types. Solubilizers and surfactants/emulsifiers enhance the solubility and stability of active pharmaceutical ingredients, facilitating better absorption. Polyols serve as humectants and stabilizers, improving product texture and moisture retention. Carbohydrates, such as sugars and starches, act as fillers and stabilize formulations. Other excipients include binders and preservatives, crucial for maintaining the integrity and efficacy of biopharmaceuticals, ensuring they remain safe and effective throughout their shelf life.</p></p>
