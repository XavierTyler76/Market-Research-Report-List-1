<p><strong>The Semiconductor Photomask Inspection Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor photomask inspection equipment market includes two primary methods: Die to Die (DD) and Die to Database (DB). The Die to Die method compares individual semiconductor die against one another to identify defects, ensuring consistency across production. In contrast, the Die to Database method involves inspecting die against a pre-established database of defect-free patterns, enabling quick identification of discrepancies in mask quality. Both methods are crucial for maintaining high standards in semiconductor manufacturing and enhancing yield rates.</p></p>
