<p><p>The automotive green tires market includes two main types: bias tires and radial tires. Bias tires are constructed with layers of cord arranged diagonally, providing flexibility and a softer ride, but generally less fuel efficiency. In contrast, radial tires feature cords arranged at right angles, offering improved fuel efficiency, better traction, and longer tread life. As the automotive industry focuses on sustainability, green tires incorporate eco-friendly materials and technologies, promoting reduced rolling resistance and enhanced performance while minimizing environmental impact.</p></p>
