<p><p>The Helmet-mounted Head-Up Display (HUD) market can be categorized into two main types: inside the helmet and outside the helmet. Inside the helmet HUDs integrate display systems directly within the helmet's visor, providing a clear view of essential information without obstructing the user's field of vision. Conversely, outside the helmet HUDs project data onto a separate screen or visor, allowing users to access information while maintaining situational awareness. Both types enhance safety and performance in various applications, including aviation, military, and motorsports.</p></p>
