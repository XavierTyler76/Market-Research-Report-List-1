<p><p>LBS (Location-Based Services) platforms are categorized into indoor and outdoor markets based on their usage environments. Indoor LBS platforms enhance navigation and location tracking within buildings, utilizing technologies like Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and RFID to provide services such as indoor mapping, asset tracking, and wayfinding. In contrast, outdoor LBS platforms leverage GPS and cellular networks to offer services for navigation, geofencing, and location-based marketing in open areas. Both types serve distinct user needs while integrating location data for enhanced experiences.</p></p>
