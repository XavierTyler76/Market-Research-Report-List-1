<p><p>T-slot cylinder sensors are used in automation and industrial applications for detecting the position of cylinders. The market includes two primary types: PNP and NPN cylinder sensors. PNP sensors provide a positive voltage output when activated, suitable for high-side switching in control circuits. In contrast, NPN sensors offer a negative voltage output, ideal for low-side switching. Both types are essential for enhancing precision in positioning systems, ensuring reliable operation in automated machinery and processes, and catering to diverse customer needs.</p></p>
