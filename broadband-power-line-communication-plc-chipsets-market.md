<p><p>Broadband Power Line Communication (PLC) chipsets are categorized into standalone and hybrid markets. Standalone chipsets operate independently, enabling devices to communicate over existing power lines without additional technologies. In contrast, hybrid chipsets combine power line communication with other technologies, such as Wi-Fi or Ethernet, to enhance data transmission and reach. This allows for greater flexibility and robustness in communication networks, catering to diverse applications and environments, while leveraging existing electrical infrastructure for seamless connectivity.</p></p>
