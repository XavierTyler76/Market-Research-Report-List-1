<p><p>The Rear View Camera Lens market primarily features two types: CCD and CMOS cameras. CCD cameras are known for their high image quality, especially in low-light conditions, making them ideal for premium applications. They consume more power and are costlier. CMOS cameras, on the other hand, are more affordable, energy-efficient, and compact, making them popular for mass-market vehicles. While CMOS cameras have improved in quality, they may still lag behind CCD in extreme lighting conditions. Both types cater to safety and convenience in automotive rear-view systems.</p></p>
