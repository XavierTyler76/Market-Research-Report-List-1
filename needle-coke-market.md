<p><p>The Needle Coke market is primarily divided into two types: petroleum-based and coal-based. Petroleum-based Needle Coke is derived from high-quality crude oil and is known for its low sulfur and high graphitization properties, making it ideal for high-performance applications like lithium-ion batteries and electrodes in electric arc furnaces. Coal-based Needle Coke, on the other hand, is produced from coking coal and tends to have higher impurities. It is mainly used in applications that require less stringent performance characteristics, like traditional electrodes.</p></p>
