<p><p>The waste management compactors market includes three main types: self-contained compactors, vertical compactors, and stationary compactors. Self-contained compactors are designed for wet waste and incorporate a built-in container for efficient disposal. Vertical compactors are tall and compact, ideal for dry waste, maximizing space in limited areas. Stationary compactors, suitable for large volumes of waste, require a separate container and are often used in industrial settings. Each type uniquely caters to diverse waste management needs, enhancing efficiency and reducing costs.</p></p>
