<p><p>DDoS protection software is available in two main types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. On-premises solutions require organizations to install software and hardware within their own facilities, providing complete control and customization over security measures. Both types aim to safeguard networks and applications from distributed denial-of-service attacks, ensuring uptime and reliability.</p></p>
