<p><p>The stone wool market comprises various types, each serving distinct insulation needs. Felt type is characterized by its flexible mat form, ideal for thermal insulation in buildings. Strip type is designed for easier installation in horizontal spaces, often used in roofs and walls. Tubular type is shaped for pipes and HVAC systems, providing effective thermal protection. Granular type consists of loose stones, suitable for filling gaps and lightweight applications, while plate type offers rigid panels for structural insulation in various constructions.</p></p>
