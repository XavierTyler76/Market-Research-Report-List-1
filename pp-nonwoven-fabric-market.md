<p><p>The PP Nonwoven Fabric market is categorized into four main types: spunbonded, staples, meltblown, and composite fabrics. Spunbonded fabrics are produced by continuously extruding and bonding fibers, offering durability and strength. Staple nonwovens are made from shorter fibers, providing softness and absorbency. Meltblown fabrics are created by melting plastic and forming fine fibers, ideal for filtration applications. Composite nonwovens combine multiple layers or types of nonwovens to enhance performance and functionality, making them versatile for various industries.</p></p>
