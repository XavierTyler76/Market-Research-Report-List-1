<p><ul><li>Open Magnetic Circuit Type Igniting Coil</li><li>Closed Magnetic Circuit Type Igniting Coil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive motor igniting coil market includes two main types: Open Magnetic Circuit Type and Closed Magnetic Circuit Type. Open Magnetic Circuit Type coils have a simpler design with magnetic flux partially escaping, making them cost-effective and suitable for standard applications. Closed Magnetic Circuit Type coils feature a more efficient design with magnetic flux fully contained, offering higher energy efficiency and performance, ideal for advanced or high-performance vehicles. Both types are essential for spark generation in internal combustion engines.</p></p>
