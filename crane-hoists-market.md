<p><p>The crane hoists market is primarily divided into two types: wire rope hoists and chain hoists. Wire rope hoists utilize strong, flexible wires for lifting heavy loads, offering durability and efficiency for industrial applications. They are ideal for high-capacity operations. Chain hoists, on the other hand, use interconnected chains and are preferred for lighter loads and portability. Typically more cost-effective, chain hoists are suitable for construction and smaller-scale operations, providing versatility in various environments.</p></p>
