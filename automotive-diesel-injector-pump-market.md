<p><p>The automotive diesel injector pump market includes several types, each serving distinct applications. Common rail diesel injection pumps deliver fuel at high pressure to multiple injectors, enhancing efficiency and reducing emissions. In-line diesel injection pumps feature a series of pumps in a line, ideal for high-performance engines. Distributor (rotary) diesel injection pumps distribute fuel to each cylinder sequentially, suitable for smaller engines. Other types encompass less common technologies or applications, catering to specialized needs within the diesel engine market.</p></p>
