<p><p>LNG as a bunker fuel market consists of three primary types: Truck to Ship (TTS), where LNG is transported via LNG fuel trucks directly to vessels at port; Port to Ship (PTS), involving the transfer of LNG from stationary terminals or ships directly to vessels moored at the port; and Ship to Ship (STS), where LNG is transferred between two ships at sea. Each method caters to different logistical needs and operational efficiencies in maritime LNG fueling.</p></p>
