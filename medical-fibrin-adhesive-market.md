<p><p>The medical fibrin adhesive market is categorized into four types: </p><p>1. **Water-based** adhesives utilize a liquid composition, making them easy to apply and less irritating to tissues. </p><p>2. **Solvent-based** adhesives contain solvents that enhance adhesion but may have concerns regarding biocompatibility. </p><p>3. **Solids** are pre-formed adhesives providing strong bonds but require specific application methods. </p><p>4. **Hot Melt-based** adhesives become tacky upon heating, offering rapid setting times and versatility in various surgical applications. Each type serves distinct medical needs, ensuring effective wound closure and tissue repair.</p></p>
