<p><strong>The Fibre Reinforced Polymer Pole Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pultrusion</li><li>Filament Winding</li><li>Centrifugal Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fibre Reinforced Polymer (FRP) pole market utilizes varied manufacturing techniques such as pultrusion, filament winding, and centrifugal casting. Pultrusion involves pulling continuous fibers through a resin bath and into a heated die for shaping, ensuring uniform cross-sections. Filament winding wraps resin-coated fibers around a rotating mandrel, allowing for high-strength structures tailored to specific designs. Centrifugal casting involves rotating a mold to distribute resin and fibers uniformly, creating dense and sturdy poles. Each method offers unique advantages, catering to diverse applications.</p></p>
