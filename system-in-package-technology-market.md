<p><p>System-in-Package (SiP) technology encompasses various packaging types, each tailored for specific performance and integration needs. 2-D IC packaging combines multiple chips on a single substrate, optimizing space and functionality. 2.5-D IC packaging involves placing chips on an interposer, enhancing interconnectivity while maintaining separate die. 3-D IC packaging stacks multiple chips vertically, minimizing footprint and improving speed. Multifunctional substrate integrated component packaging integrates passive and active components on a single substrate, enabling compact designs and efficient performance in advanced electronics.</p></p>
