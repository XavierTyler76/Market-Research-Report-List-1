<p><ul><li>Plasma Lighter</li><li>Electric Arc Lighter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric lighter market is primarily divided into two types: plasma lighters and electric arc lighters. Plasma lighters utilize high-voltage arcs to create a windproof flame, making them ideal for outdoor use. They are rechargeable and eco-friendly. Electric arc lighters, on the other hand, generate a dual arc to ignite materials quickly and efficiently. These lighters also feature rechargeable batteries and are designed for durability and convenience. Both types appeal to consumers seeking modern, reusable alternatives to traditional lighters.</p></p>
