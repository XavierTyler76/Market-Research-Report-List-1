<p><p>The gene detection market consists of several key types, including biochemical tests, chromosome analysis, and DNA analysis. Biochemical tests identify specific biomarkers using laboratory techniques to assess genetic conditions or diseases. Chromosome analysis, or karyotyping, examines the number and structure of chromosomes to detect abnormalities associated with genetic disorders. DNA analysis involves sequencing or amplifying specific DNA segments to investigate genetic variations or mutations, enabling the diagnosis of inherited diseases and aiding in personalized medicine approaches.</p></p>
