<p><strong>The NDT (Non-Destructive Testing) Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic, Radiographic</li><li>Eddy Current</li><li>Magnetic particle</li><li>Acoustic Emission</li><li>Terahertz Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) services market includes various techniques to evaluate materials without causing damage. Ultrasonic testing uses high-frequency sound waves to detect flaws, while radiographic testing employs X-rays or gamma rays for internal inspection. Eddy current testing examines conductive materials for surface defects using electromagnetic induction. Magnetic particle testing identifies surface and near-surface imperfections in ferromagnetic materials. Acoustic emission detects stress waves from defects, and terahertz imaging analyzes materials at submillimeter wavelengths for structural integrity.</p></p>
