<p><strong>The Chemical Mechanical Polishing CMP Diamond Pad Regulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plated Type</li><li>Brazed Type</li><li>Sintered Type</li><li>CVD Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Chemical Mechanical Polishing (CMP) diamond pad regulator market encompasses four primary types: plated, brazed, sintered, and chemical vapor deposition (CVD) types. Plated type regulators feature a layer of diamond particles bonded to a substrate for improved polishing efficiency. Brazed types use a brazing alloy to secure diamond segments, enhancing durability. Sintered types involve compacting diamond powders at high temperatures, resulting in robust pads. CVD types utilize a process to grow diamonds on a substrate, offering precision and consistent quality for semiconductor applications.</p></p>
