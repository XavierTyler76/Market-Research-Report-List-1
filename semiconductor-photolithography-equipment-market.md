<p><p>The semiconductor photolithography equipment market encompasses three primary types: UV, DUV, and EUV. UV (Ultraviolet) lithography uses traditional light sources for patterning but is limited in resolution. DUV (Deep Ultraviolet) lithography employs shorter wavelengths, enabling finer feature resolutions, making it crucial for advanced nodes. EUV (Extreme Ultraviolet) lithography uses even shorter wavelengths to achieve exceptional precision, essential for the latest manufacturing technologies. Each type plays a vital role in advancing semiconductor fabrication capabilities.</p></p>
