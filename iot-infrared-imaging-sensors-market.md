<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The IoT Infrared Imaging Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short Wave Infrared Imaging</li><li>Medium Wave Infrared Imaging</li><li>Long Wave Infrared Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IoT infrared imaging sensors market comprises three key types: </p><p>1. **Short-Wave Infrared (SWIR)** sensors, which operate in the 0.9 to 1.7 micrometer range, are effective for environmental monitoring and materials sorting. </p><p>2. **Medium-Wave Infrared (MWIR)** sensors, functioning between 3 to 5 micrometers, excel in military and surveillance applications due to their sensitivity to heat sources. </p><p>3. **Long-Wave Infrared (LWIR)** sensors, in the 8 to 14 micrometer range, are ideal for thermal imaging, detecting body heat, and various industrial applications. Each type caters to specific usage requirements in IoT ecosystems.</p></p>
