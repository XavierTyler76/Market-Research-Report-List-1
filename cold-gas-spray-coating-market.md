<p><p>The Cold Gas Spray Coating market is segmented into high-pressure and low-pressure systems based on the operational pressure used in the spraying process. High-pressure cold gas spray employs elevated pressures to accelerate particles, resulting in denser coatings with better adhesion and mechanical properties. In contrast, low-pressure cold gas spray operates at lower pressures, favoring delicate substrates and intricate geometries, offering more control over coating thickness and minimizing thermal stress. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications, enhancing surface performance and durability.</p></p>
