<p><p>The vehicle lighting tool market comprises three primary types: Halogen, HID (High-Intensity Discharge), and LED (Light Emitting Diode) lighting tools. Halogen lighting tools are traditional, providing bright light but consuming more energy. HID lighting tools offer improved brightness and efficiency, ideal for high-performance applications. In contrast, LED lighting tools are gaining popularity due to their energy efficiency, longevity, and lower heat output, making them a sustainable choice for modern vehicles. Each type serves different preferences and requirements in automotive lighting.</p></p>
