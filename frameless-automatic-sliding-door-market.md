<p><p>The frameless automatic sliding door market features two main types: foot pedal and photoelectric. Foot pedal types allow users to open doors hands-free by activating a sensor with their foot, making them ideal for high-traffic areas. In contrast, photoelectric types use infrared sensors to detect movement, automatically opening as someone approaches. Both types enhance accessibility and convenience, catering to various environments such as commercial buildings, hospitals, and shopping centers, by improving the flow of people while maintaining aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
