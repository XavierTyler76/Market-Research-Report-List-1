<p><p>The spin bike market is primarily categorized into two types based on resistance mechanisms: magnetic resistance and felt pad resistance. </p><p>Magnetic resistance bikes utilize magnets to create resistance, offering a smooth and quiet ride with adjustable settings, making them ideal for home use. In contrast, felt pad resistance bikes employ friction pads against the flywheel, allowing for a more traditional cycling experience. While they can provide a challenging workout, they tend to be noisier and require more maintenance. Each type caters to different user preferences and performance needs.</p></p>
